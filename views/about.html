About the creator

Melissa dela Merced is a technology designer, educator, and filmmaker from Quezon City, Philippines. She's an advocate for free speech on the internet and government transparency. Co-founder of taopo.org, a website dedicated to providing Filipinos an online space from social media movements to real action. She has spent more than 10 years in television in broadcast technology and design. She has taught classes and workshops at the University of the Philippines Film Institute. A frequent traveller and photographer, she's also an avid gamer and comic book collector
