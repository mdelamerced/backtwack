About Entwines

Entwines is a thesis project from New York University's Interactive Telecommunications Program at the Tisch School of the Arts.

Entwines is built upon the idea of aggregating the voice of many to present to the few.

About the author

Melissa dela Merced is a technology designer, educator, and filmmaker from Manila, Philippines. She has more than 10 years broadcasting experience in broadcast technology and design. She has also taught classes at the University of the Philippines Film Institute. She's also an avid gamer and comic book collector
